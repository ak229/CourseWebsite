== Form a Group and choose a Project (1 point)

Submit project proposal along with your group members. Only one member of the group needs to submit. Individuals will only get points if they are in a group that has been submitted.

You have freedom to choose your group members and project idea under two conditions:

  • Groups must have at least 3 members.
  • Projects must be substantial enough to justify a group of seniors working for an entire semester.
== Create a Slack Channels (1 Point)

Create a public slack channel for your group on CSE Software (https://cse-software.slack.com/) and add a short channel description listing your group members and a description of your projects. This will be your primary channel for group communication.

Also create a private slack channel and add your group members and course staff to the channel. This private channel will only be used for sensitive communication with the course staff.

Slack will be used by the course staff for all communication regarding this course. You are responsible for the information posted on slack.

It is highly recommended that you download and install a client for slack and turn on notifications.
